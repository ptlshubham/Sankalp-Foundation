About Sankalp Foundation

Welcome to Sankalp Foundation

Sankalp Foundation was founded on 2016 by a small group of young friends with a strong passion to change society as it is today and build a better India for tomorrow. Sankalp Foundation formally registered under Societies Registration Act - XXI of 1860 during the year 2016 & Trust Registration (Act of 1950/29) F/2576/GNR.

Education, social welfare, social awareness, environment, health have been covered under all the initiatives taken by Sankalp foundation.
