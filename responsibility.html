Terms And Condition Applied To Every JCSM Centre

The process of the renewal, advertisement and the conditions of the renewals of the JCSM, Centre is very unique. Therefore, following terms and condition are to be followed JCSM. Centre.

  • For new renewal center must applied one month before the expiry of last renewal.
  • Every center must pay 1000/- Rs. To the Institution for the advertisement in News paper through which advertisement of all local centers can be made twice in the year.
  • The criteria of new renewal for the centre are as follows :
  • Minimum 100 Students for free course in one Year.
  • Minimum 50 Students for Paid course in one Year.
  • Minimum 250 Students for any free course in one Year.
  • Minimum 80 Students for and Paid course in one Year.

If your centre is unable to produce the students according to the above mentioned criteria then the decision of next renewal for the centre will be taken by the committee.

We hope that you all will try hard to fullfill the above criteria and make more students for Saksharta in the field of computers.
